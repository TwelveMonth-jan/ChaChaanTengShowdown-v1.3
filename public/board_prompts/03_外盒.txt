Component Name: 遊戲外盒 (Game Outer Box)
Category: 包裝 (Packaging)
Quantity: x1
Dimensions: 295mm x 295mm x 70mm (standard square board game box, fits A3-folded board)

---

Theme: Chaotic cha chaan teng lunch rush captured in a single hero illustration on the lid. The back and sides of the box give players a feel for the game at a glance — characters, chaos, and the core hook (survival + lying + sabotage).

Art Style: Exploding Kittens style — bold black outlines, exaggerated cartoon, vibrant flat colors, humorous and quirky, slightly absurd, hand-drawn feel. Hero-shot composition on the lid.

=== LID TOP (蓋面) ===

Prompt:
A chaotic hero-shot illustration of a Hong Kong cha chaan teng at peak lunch hour, drawn in Exploding Kittens art style. The scene is packed and loud. In the foreground, the six playable characters are all visible simultaneously doing absurd things: the Rookie Waiter (阿志) is in the middle panicking with a tray of milk teas tilting dangerously; the Veteran (發哥) is off to one side smirking with a pencil behind his ear; the Kitchen Boss (肥威) is behind a pass-through window with flames shooting from his wok; the Cashier Auntie (蓮姐) is at a counter in the back-right holding a stack of cash; the Delivery Boy (小強) is running out the left side with a tower of takeaway boxes; the Floor Star (阿May) is winking while balancing plates. Angry customers at tables are banging chopsticks, a food critic is scribbling on a notebook, a KOL is live-streaming. Food flies through the air — pineapple buns, egg tarts, bowls of noodles, cups of milk tea splashing. Bold black outlines, vibrant flat colors (dominant reds, yellows, teals, cream). Lots of speed lines, sweat drops, exclamation marks, and cartoon panic symbols.

Title Treatment:
- Main title: "茶餐廳風雲" in huge bold handwritten-style Chinese calligraphy across the top, with thick black outline and dripping red color (like soy sauce or chili oil)
- English subtitle below: "CHA CHAAN TENG BRAWL" in bold condensed block letters
- Tagline: "午市生存大亂鬥 — 3-5 players · 8+ · 20-30 min"
- Small player count / age / time icons in the bottom corners
