Component Name: 中央板 (Central Board)
Category: 遊戲主板 (Main Game Board)
Quantity: x1
Dimensions: A3 (420mm x 297mm), folds in half to A4 for storage

---

Theme: Top-down cutaway view of a chaotic Hong Kong cha chaan teng (tea restaurant) during lunch rush. The board is both atmospheric artwork AND functional playing surface — zones are embedded diegetically into the restaurant scene.

Art Style: Exploding Kittens style — bold black outlines, exaggerated cartoon, vibrant flat colors, humorous and quirky, slightly absurd, hand-drawn feel. Top-down perspective with slight isometric tilt so objects still look readable.

---

Prompt:
A top-down cutaway illustration of a chaotic 1980s-style Hong Kong cha chaan teng interior, drawn in Exploding Kittens art style. The restaurant fills the entire board. Visible elements include: a long open kitchen along the top edge with a sweaty chef flipping woks and flames shooting up; a red-and-white tiled floor with black grout lines; a cashier counter in the bottom-right corner with a vintage cash register and a small pile of coins; a red plastic stool row at the left entrance doorway where grumpy customers are queuing; a washing sink area in the bottom-left full of dirty dishes and soap bubbles; a ceiling fan spinning; fluorescent tube lights; handwritten Chinese paper menus taped on the back wall; a big red wall clock; framed photos of old Hong Kong celebrities on the walls. The whole scene is drawn with bold black outlines, vibrant flat colors (reds, yellows, teals, creams), and humorous exaggeration. Leave clearly-bordered rectangular slots within the scene for card placement — these slots should look like they're part of the restaurant (e.g., the kitchen pass-through window IS the deck slot).

Zone Details:

1. 🏪 **中央牌庫 (Deck Slot)** — upper-left
   - Functional: holds the shuffled central deck
   - Diegetic framing: drawn as the kitchen pass-through window, with order tickets clipped above it
   - Slot size: ~70mm x 95mm (fits sleeved poker cards)

2. 📋 **奧客排隊區 (Nasty Customer Queue)** — upper-right
   - PURELY VISUAL / FLAVOR — no mechanical function
   - Drawn as a row of red plastic stools at the restaurant entrance with 3–4 grumpy cartoon customers waiting
   - Players may optionally set aside 奧客 cards here for thematic flavor, but rules don't require it

3. 🗑️ **棄牌區 (Discard Pile)** — lower-left
   - Functional: holds the discard pile
   - Diegetic framing: drawn as a washing sink overflowing with dirty dishes, chopsticks, soap bubbles
   - Slot size: ~70mm x 95mm

4. 🕐 **回合指示軌 (Turn Indicator)** — lower-right
   - Functional: tracks current turn player with a physical marker (a small wok token)
   - Diegetic framing: drawn as a big red round wall clock with Chinese numerals. Around the clock face, 5 small waiter-head icons represent player seats (for up to 5 players). A clockwise arrow reminds turn order.

5. ℹ️ **快速規則卡 (Quick Reference)** — center back wall
   - Drawn as a handwritten paper menu taped to the wall
   - Content:
     * 回合流程：1.抽牌 → 2.行動 → 3.查勤 → 4.奧客結算
     * 即時淘汰：怒氣 ≥ 抗壓 立刻出局
     * 踢爆結算：抓包→出牌者+1怒氣；抓錯→踢爆者+1怒氣

Color Theme: dominant warm reds and creams with teal accents. Slight yellowing/aging for retro feel.

Printing Notes:
- Full bleed 3mm all sides
- Matte finish recommended (reduces glare during play)
- Fold line down the middle (vertical) with score mark
- Board thickness: 2mm greyboard laminated both sides
