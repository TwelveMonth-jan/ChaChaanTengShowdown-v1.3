Art Style: Exploding Kittens style — bold black outlines, exaggerated cartoon, vibrant flat colors, humorous and quirky, slightly absurd, hand-drawn feel.

A cartoon sunny-side-up fried egg, drawn in Exploding Kittens art style. The egg has a chill relaxed face on the yolk with half-closed cool eyes and a zen smile, like a yoga master. The egg white is wobbly and slightly crispy at the edges with brown crispy bits. The yolk is a perfect bright orange-yellow dome, almost glowing. Small oil sizzle bubbles around the edges. Bold black outlines around everything. Background: warm golden yellow (#FEF3C7). The egg is on a hot sizzling plate with oil droplets popping.
