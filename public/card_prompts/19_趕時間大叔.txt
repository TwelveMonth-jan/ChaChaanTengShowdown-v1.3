Card Name: 趕時間大叔 (Rushing Uncle)
Category: 奧客卡 (Difficult Customer Card)
Quantity: x1
Requires: 奶茶 (Milk Tea) + 公仔麵 (Instant Noodles) + 煎蛋 (Fried Egg)
Tip: 2
Special: 🔥 催單 — 每回合結束 +1 怒氣
Quote: 「我趕住去開會！三分鐘搞掂佢！」

---

Art Style: Exploding Kittens style — bold black outlines, exaggerated cartoon, vibrant flat colors, humorous and quirky, slightly absurd, hand-drawn feel.

Prompt:
A cartoon middle-aged businessman in a rush, drawn in Exploding Kittens art style. He's wearing a suit but everything is disheveled — tie flying backward, jacket half-off. He has three watches on one arm all showing different times, and he's tapping all of them frantically. His face is pure stress with gritted teeth and one eye twitching. He's half-standing half-sitting, already grabbing his bag to leave before the food even arrives. A giant stopwatch above his head counting down "3:00". Bold black outlines around everything. Background: angry red/pink (#FECACA). Motion blur lines on everything to show impatience.

Card Layout:
- Top: Card title "趕時間大叔" in bold Chinese characters with small English subtitle "RUSHING UNCLE" directly beneath (about 40% the title size), background light red
- Center: The illustration (main focus, ~55% of card area)
- Bottom left: Required ingredients panel — clearly render all THREE required food icons with its Chinese name above and English name below in smaller text. Must show: 🍵 奶茶 / Milk Tea  +  🍜 公仔麵 / Instant Noodles  +  🍳 煎蛋 / Fried Egg. Icons must be large, recognizable, labeled, and visually separated with "+" signs. Do NOT omit or abbreviate any ingredient.
- Bottom right: Tip value "💰 小費 2 / Tip 2" + "🔥 催單 / Rushing" badge
- Footer: smaller italic English line "Always in a rush — anger +1 each turn"
- Border: Thick bold black border with rounded corners
- Color theme: Light red / urgent orange

Dimensions: 63mm x 88mm (standard poker card size)
