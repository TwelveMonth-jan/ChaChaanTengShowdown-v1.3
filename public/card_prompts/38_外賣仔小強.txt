Card Name: 外賣仔 小強 (Delivery Boy Siu Keung)
Category: 角色卡 (Character Card)
Quantity: x1

Stats:
- 🔥 抗壓: 4
- ✋ 手勢: 6
- 🪑 桌位: 3

Passive 被動「飛毛腿」:
查勤階段，不論有多少張空桌，你最多只增加 2 點怒氣值（空桌懲罰封頂為 2）。

Active 主動技能「飛單」(每回合 1 次):
將自己桌位區中 1 位客人移至自己另一張空桌（若該客人是奧客，此動作會重置其當回合催單效果）。

Quote: 「讓開讓開！外賣到！唔該借借！」

---

Art Style: Exploding Kittens style — bold black outlines, exaggerated cartoon, vibrant flat colors, humorous and quirky, slightly absurd, hand-drawn feel.

Prompt:
A cartoon skinny Hong Kong cha chaan teng delivery boy character, drawn in Exploding Kittens art style. A lean energetic young man wearing a red delivery vest over a white t-shirt, a backwards baseball cap, and worn sneakers. He's mid-sprint, leaning forward dramatically, carrying a huge stack of styrofoam takeaway boxes balanced precariously in both arms, with plastic bags of drinks dangling from his wrists. His tongue sticks out slightly with focus and his legs blur with speed. One shoelace untied flying behind him. Bold black outlines. Background: bright lime green (#84CC16). Strong comic speed lines radiating behind him.
