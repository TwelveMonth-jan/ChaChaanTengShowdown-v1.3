Card Name: 隔夜飯 (Overnight Rice)
Category: 垃圾卡 (Trash Card)
Quantity: x2
Quote: 「點解啲飯硬過石頭？」

---

Art Style: Exploding Kittens style — bold black outlines, exaggerated cartoon, vibrant flat colors, humorous and quirky, slightly absurd, hand-drawn feel.

Prompt:
A cartoon bowl of stale overnight rice, drawn in Exploding Kittens art style. The rice has hardened into a solid rock-like lump shaped vaguely like a frowning grumpy face with cracked dry surface. The rice grains are grey and crusty instead of fluffy white. A pair of chopsticks is stuck in it bent at a crazy angle because the rice is too hard. Tiny cracks and dust particles floating off. A tooth chipped on the rice sits beside the bowl. Bold black outlines around everything. Background: soft purple (#EDE9FE). Stink lines and "zzz" symbols showing it's been sitting out.

Card Layout:
- Top: Card title "隔夜飯" in bold Chinese characters with small English subtitle "OVERNIGHT RICE" directly beneath (about 40% the title size), background light purple
- Center: The illustration (main focus, ~70% of card area)
- Bottom: Flavor text "點解啲飯硬過石頭？", followed by a smaller italic English line "Why is the rice harder than a rock?"
- Border: Thick bold black border with rounded corners
- Color theme: Light purple / stale grey

Dimensions: 63mm x 88mm (standard poker card size)
