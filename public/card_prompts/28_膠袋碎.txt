Art Style: Exploding Kittens style — bold black outlines, exaggerated cartoon, vibrant flat colors, humorous and quirky, slightly absurd, hand-drawn feel.

Prompt:
A cartoon crumpled torn red plastic bag piece mixed into a bowl of food, drawn in Exploding Kittens art style. The plastic bag shreds have tiny mischievous faces, sticking out from under the noodles. The red-and-white striped classic Hong Kong plastic bag pattern is visible. One piece of plastic is wrapped around a chopstick like it's trying to escape. A small printed logo "多謝惠顧" (Thank You for Your Patronage) is partially visible on one piece. Bold black outlines around everything. Background: soft purple (#EDE9FE). Wrinkle and crinkle effect lines.

Card Layout:
- Top: Card title "膠袋碎" in bold Chinese characters with small English subtitle "PLASTIC BAG BITS" directly beneath (about 40% the title size), background light purple
- Center: The illustration (main focus, ~70% of card area)
- Bottom: Flavor text "食到一嚿膠⋯你認真？", followed by a smaller italic English line "I just bit into plastic... seriously?"
- Border: Thick bold black border with rounded corners
- Color theme: Light purple / plastic red-white

Dimensions: 63mm x 88mm (standard poker card size)
