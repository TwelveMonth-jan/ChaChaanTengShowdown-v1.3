Component Name: 個人桌墊 (Player Mat)
Category: 個人遊戲區 (Personal Play Area)
Quantity: x5 (one universal design, printed 5 copies for up to 5 players)
Dimensions: A5 landscape (210mm x 148mm)

---

Theme: A single restaurant table viewed top-down, with a red-and-white checkered tablecloth. Functional zones (tables, anger track, tip track, character slot, hand area) are printed onto the tablecloth as if they were placemats, menus, and napkins.

Art Style: Exploding Kittens style — bold black outlines, cartoon, vibrant flat colors. Top-down perspective. The mat is universal (same art for all 6 characters).

---

Prompt:
A top-down illustration of a Hong Kong cha chaan teng table with a classic red-and-white checkered plastic tablecloth covering the entire mat, drawn in Exploding Kittens art style. Printed onto the tablecloth (as if they were paper placemats and napkins) are functional zones — 3 square "table slots" for customer cards, an "anger thermometer" running along the top edge, a row of 6 "tip coin circles" along the bottom, a "character card slot" in the upper-left corner, and a "hand area" label along the bottom. Everything is drawn with bold black outlines and a hand-drawn feel. Tiny decorative touches around the edges: a pair of wooden chopsticks in a paper wrapper, a small bottle of soy sauce, a cup ring stain, a used napkin, a few sesame seeds. The 3rd table slot is drawn with a dashed outline and half-transparent fill, with small text "⚠ 小強專用" to indicate it's only usable by the Delivery Boy character. The 1st table slot has a small gold frame with "⭐ 肥威專用 (僅此 1 張)" to indicate it's the only slot used by the Kitchen Boss character.

Zone Details:

1. **角色卡插槽 (Character Card Slot)** — upper-left
   - Size: 63mm x 88mm (fits poker-sized character card)
   - Drawn as a small menu-holder or name-card stand on the tablecloth
   - Placing the chosen character card here keeps its 3 stats (抗壓/手勢/桌位) always visible

2. **🔥 怒氣軌 (Anger Track)** — top edge
   - 7 steps: 0, 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6 (6 exists for 發哥 whose 抗壓 = 6)
   - Drawn as a cartoon thermometer with the boss's angry face at the top
   - Use a wooden cube or small marker to track current anger value
   - Step 5 has a bold red line labeled "⚠ 預設淘汰線"; step 6 has a smaller line labeled "發哥淘汰線"

3. **🪑 桌位 1–3 (Table Slots)** — center row
   - Three 70mm x 95mm slots side by side
   - **Slot 1**: solid border with a small gold frame, label "⭐ 肥威專用 (僅此 1 張)" — indicates this is the only slot used by 後廚大佬 肥威
   - **Slot 2**: solid border, standard — used by everyone with 桌位 ≥ 2
   - **Slot 3**: DASHED border with half-transparent fill, label "⚠ 小強專用" — only usable by 外賣仔 小強 (桌位 = 3)
   - Below each slot: a smaller dashed rectangle labeled "[食材區]" for placing ingredient cards committed to that customer during 上餐

4. **💰 小費 (Tip Track)** — bottom-left
   - 6 empty circles (drawn as coin outlines) labeled "小費"
   - Players place tip tokens (cardboard coins) on filled circles
   - If 6 is exceeded, use a second row or extra tokens off-mat

5. **✋ 手牌區 (Hand Area)** — bottom-right (decorative label only)
   - Just a labeled decorative zone indicating where the player should hold/place their hand cards face-down when not in use
   - Drawn as an open paper menu or a napkin with chopsticks

Color Theme: red-and-white checkered tablecloth dominant. Zones drawn in cream/off-white rectangles with thick black borders so they pop against the red squares.

Printing Notes:
- Full bleed 3mm all sides
- Matte or linen finish (feels like a real tablecloth)
- Mat thickness: 1.5mm or thick cardstock (300gsm+)
- Same file printed 5x for 5-player game
